# Casablanca


## Ingredients

* 2 oz Light Rum
* 1.5 oz Apricot Lady
* 1 oz Lime Juice
* 1 oz Grand Marnier
* 1 tsp Maraschino Liqueur
* 1 tsp St. Germain
* 1/2 tsp powdered sugar

## Instructions

1. Combine ingredients in a shaker over ice
2. Stir until combined and well chilled
3. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass
